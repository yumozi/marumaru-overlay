# Privacy Policy — Marumaru 日文歌词浮窗显示 Japanese Lyrics Overlay

**Last updated:** March 15, 2026

## Summary

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Analytics or usage data

## Local Storage

The extension uses `chrome.storage.local` to save your preferences on your device only:
- Overlay on/off state
- Font sizes (lyrics and translation)
- Text color
- Overlay position

This data never leaves your browser.

## Website Access

The extension accesses `marumaru-x.com` song pages solely to read the currently playing lyric line. No data from the site is stored or transmitted to any external server.

## Third Parties

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/your-username/marumaru-overlay).
